# Bonus Module: The EP Knowledge Base

## What We're Doing

You now have access to something no other ecommerce training gives you: a searchable, AI-powered archive of 283+ eCommerce Podcast episodes, tagged against the Slingshot Framework and ready for SAM to teach from.

This isn't just "a list of episodes." Every episode has been tagged with the Slingshot areas it covers, the FUEL marketing stage it applies to, and a one-sentence key takeaway. When you ask SAM a question, it can now pull in real-world evidence from founders, agency experts, and practitioners who've been on the show.

## What's In the Archive

283+ episodes, each tagged with:

- **Slingshot areas** — which of the 7 framework areas it covers (Sell, Story, Tech Stack, Marketing, Optimise, Experience, Growth)
- **FUEL stages** — the marketing maturity level (Foundation, Unlock, Elevate, Leapfrog)
- **Website menu tags** — specific topics from the EP website taxonomy
- **Key takeaway** — the single most actionable insight in one sentence
- **Related skills** — which SlingshotAI skills connect to the content

## How to Use It

### Find episodes about a topic

```
What EP episodes cover referral marketing?
```

Claude searches the index and returns the most relevant episodes with their key takeaways and Slingshot area mapping.

### Get evidence for a framework concept

```
sam, teach me about the Growth area of Slingshot. What evidence is there from the podcast?
```

SAM teaches the Growth methodology from its own learn content, then pulls in specific episodes as real-world examples: "Jay Myers on EP 283 showed that a 0.4 increase in referral rate produced 56x subscriber growth — that's the viral coefficient in action."

### Find episodes by FUEL stage

```
Show me Foundation-level episodes about email marketing
```

Great for when you know you need to get the basics right before trying advanced tactics.

### Find episodes by guest or company

```
What episodes feature someone from Shopify?
```

### Connect episodes to your audit results

```
My Moby score was low on trust signals. Are there any EP episodes about building trust on product pages?
```

Claude finds episodes tagged with both `moby` as a related skill and Optimise as a Slingshot area.

## Why This Matters

Most ecommerce founders listen to podcasts passively — they hear something useful, think "I should do that," and then forget it by Tuesday. The EP Knowledge Base turns passive listening into an active knowledge system.

When SAM teaches you about mobile conversion, it doesn't just explain the theory — it references the specific episode where Adam Pearce from Blend Commerce explained why your search feature matters more than your homepage. When you run a Brand Voice audit, SAM can point you to the episode where Innocent Drinks' approach to tone of voice was discussed.

The archive gets more valuable every week as new episodes are published and tagged.
