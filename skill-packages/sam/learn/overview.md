# The Slingshot Framework — Overview

The Slingshot Framework is the methodology that underpins everything in SlingshotAI. It's a system built from decades of experience in building and selling ecommerce businesses, focused on practical steps that create real value.

## The Seven Areas

Slingshot has seven interconnected parts. Every ecommerce business needs all seven working:

1. **Sell** — Constant curation of high-demand products that generate profit. Uses the Product Quadrant (Knowledge vs Demand) to evaluate and improve products. The goal: move every product toward high demand + high knowledge.

2. **Story** — Your brand messaging only matters where your story and your customer's story overlap. Uses the Story Overlap (Venn diagram) to find the intersection. Key diagnostic: the We vs You ratio on any page.

3. **Tech Stack** — Your website, email platform, CMS, and all associated technology must work together to provide a familiar buying experience. Mobile-first. Simple. Integrated.

4. **Marketing** — Eight pillars of digital marketing: Email, Mobile, Paid Media, Search, Content, Referral & Partnerships, Social, Feedback & Strategy. Uses the FUEL framework (Foundation → Unlock → Elevate → Leapfrog) to build marketing maturity across all eight areas.

5. **Optimise** — Increasing the chances of a visitor buying. Three pillars: Easy to find (navigation), Find the answer (product pages as mini-websites), Clear CTA. Everything tested and backed by data.

6. **Experience** — The entire post-purchase journey. From the thank-you page to transactional emails to the unboxing moment. The goal: turn a routine delivery into a "wow" that drives repeat business and referrals.

7. **Growth** — Building flywheel-type growth by maximising three variables: Number of Customers, Average Order Value, and Customer Order Frequency (repeatability). Strategies include upselling, bundling, loyalty programmes, and referral systems.

## Two Guiding Principles

Everything in Slingshot is filtered through two questions:

1. **Does it move the needle?** — Prioritise tasks with the biggest impact on growth.
2. **Can I consistently hit an 8 out of 10 executing this?** — Choose actions that are sustainable and high quality, not everything at once.

## The FUEL Framework (Marketing Detail)

FUEL is the marketing-specific methodology within Slingshot. It stands for Foundation → Unlock → Elevate → Leapfrog — four progressive stages of marketing maturity worked through sequentially across all eight marketing areas.

- **Foundation** — The boring stuff that must work: email sequences, baseline metrics, conversion essentials.
- **Unlock** — Diversify. Test new channels so you're not vulnerable to one platform.
- **Elevate** — Optimise what's working. Advanced segmentation, CRO, LTV modelling.
- **Leapfrog** — Experiment boldly. AI innovations, emerging platforms, interactive experiences.

The power is in the 4x8 matrix: every marketing area at every FUEL stage. Most businesses are strong in 2-3 areas and neglect the rest.
